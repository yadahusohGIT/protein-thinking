A cheaper way to hit a protein target

I built a two-stage optimisation model to choose a low-cost weekly food basket and spread it across seven days.

How it works

Two separate decisions

1 · Choose what to buy

The first model minimises weekly cost while meeting the nutrition, variety and preparation constraints.

2 · Split it between days

The second model keeps the basket fixed and tries to make the seven days reasonably even.

3 · Change the assumptions

The four scenarios and protein sensitivity test show what happens when the constraints become stricter.
